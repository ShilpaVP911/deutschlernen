Conjunction Cheat Sheet

Distinguishing between ob, falls, and wenn is crucial for the B1 exam.

Conjunction English Best Use Case
Ob Whether Indirect Yes/No questions. No condition involved.
Falls In case Conditions with low probability.
Wenn If / When General conditions or recurring time events.

Quick Rules

  • The "Whether" Test: If you can replace "if" with "whether" in English, use ob.
  • The "Probability" Test: If the condition is unlikely or you want to say "just in case", use falls.
  • The "Default" Test: For most "if" situations and "when" situations, use wenn.

Examples

Ich weiß nicht, ob er kommt. (I don't know whether he's coming.)

Falls es regnet, nehmen wir einen Schirm. (In case it rains, we'll take an umbrella.)

Wenn ich Zeit habe, lese ich ein Buch. (If/When I have time, I read a book.)
