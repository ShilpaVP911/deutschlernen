# Learning Record: 0001-lesson-1-ob

## Context
The user is an intermediate (B1) student preparing for an exam. This record captures the initial lesson on the conjunction "ob".

## Insights
- "Ob" is often the first hurdle because English overuses "if".
- The "Whether Test" is a highly effective mnemonic for English speakers.
- B1 students still struggle with verb-last word order in subordinate clauses; this was emphasized in the lesson.
