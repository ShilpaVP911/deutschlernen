Cheat Sheet: German Verb Positions

Understanding where the verb goes is 80% of mastering German grammar. Here are the three main positions:

1. Main Clause (Aussagesatz)

Rule: The conjugated verb is in Position 2.

Example: Ich gehe heute nach Hause.

2. Question (W-Frage)

Rule: The conjugated verb is in Position 2 (after the question word).

Example: Wann gehst du nach Hause?

3. Subordinate Clause (Nebensatz)

Rule: The conjugated verb moves to the End Position.

Triggered by conjunctions like: dass, weil, wenn, obwohl.

Example: Ich weiß, dass du nach Hause gehst.
