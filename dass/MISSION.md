# Mission: Master German 'dass' Clauses

## Why
Pass the B1 German exam. Correct verb placement in subordinate clauses is a key requirement for achieving B1 proficiency and communicating nuanced thoughts.

## Success looks like
- Correctly placing the conjugated verb at the end of every 'dass' clause.
- Handling modal verbs and separable verbs within 'dass' clauses.
- Confidently using 'dass' to express opinions and facts in writing and speaking.

## Constraints
- Level: B1 Intermediate.
- Focus on exam-relevant accuracy.

## Out of scope
- Other subordinate conjunctions (weil, wenn, obwohl) for now, unless needed for comparison.
- Highly poetic or archaic structures.
