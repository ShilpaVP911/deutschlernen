# German 'dass' Clauses Resources

## Knowledge

- [Deutsche Welle (DW) - Learn German (Nicos Weg B1)](https://learngerman.dw.com/en/b1-course/s-61409341)
  Interactive course with videos and exercises. Excellent for B1 exam preparation. Use for: subordinate clause practice and listening comprehension.
- [Easy-Deutsch: Dass-Sätze](https://easy-deutsch.com/nebensaetze/dass-saetze/)
  Clear visual explanations of the "Verb-Kick" rule. Use for: understanding sentence structure diagrams.
- [YourDailyGerman: The difference between 'das' and 'dass'](https://yourdailygerman.com/das-dass-difference/)
  Deep dive into the common confusion between the relative pronoun and the conjunction. Use for: mastering spelling and usage.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/German](https://reddit.com/r/German)
  A community for learners of German. Use for: asking specific grammar questions or clarifying tricky sentence structures.
- [Goethe-Institut Community](https://www.goethe.de/en/spr/kup/msm.html)
  Official community for learners aiming for Goethe exams (A1-C2). Use for: exam-specific tips and finding study partners.
